Enzymes

Chapter 9 - Enzymes / Enzymes

  • Enzymes are a protein, biological catalyst produced by cells (on the ribosomes)
  • Enzymes are specific i.e. they only work on one particular reaction or substrate (specificity)
  • Control Metabolic Reactions
  • They are affected by pH and by temperature
  • Optimum is the condition at which they work best (not fastest). Beyond this their activity decreases
  • Eventually they become denatured as their shape changes permanently and they become inactive.
