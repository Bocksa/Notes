Chapter 9 - Enzymes / Metabolism, Photosynthesis, & Respiration

Metabolism is the sum of all the chemical reactions in a cell or organism

Solar Energy is the source of all living material through photosynthesis which builds up plant
material which in turn supplies the raw materials and energy needed by animals.

Cellular energy is the energy stored and released by the cell to carry out its functions.
