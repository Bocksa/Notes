Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Main Elements

  • Human nutrition involves the following four processes:
    1. Ingestion is the taking of food into the digestive system
    2. Digestion is the breakdown of food
      • Physical digestion is the mechanical breakdown of food.
      • Chemical digestion is the breakdown of food using enzymes
    3. Absorption occurs when the digested food passes from the digestive system and enters the blood
    4. Egestion is the removal of unabsorbed waste from the digestive system
