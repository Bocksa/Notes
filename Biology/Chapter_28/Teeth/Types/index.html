Types of Teeth

Teeth / Types of Teeth

  • Incisors are found at the front of the mouth. (Cuts and Slices)
  • Canines are the long, pointed teeth. (Grips and Tears)
  • Premolars have large, flat surfaces. (Crush and Grind)
  • Molars are the large teeth located at te back of the jaw. (Crush and grind)
