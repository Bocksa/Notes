Nutrition

Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Nutrition

  • Nutrition is the way in which an organism gets its food


    • Types:

    • Autotrophs make their own food. Plants are autotrophic, as they make their own food via Photosynthesis.
    • Heterotrophs take in food from the environment. Animals, fungi and most bacteria are heterotophic.

      • Heterotrophs:

      • Parasites take in their food from a live source
      • Saphrophytes take in their food from a dead source


    • Heterotrophs are also classified as herbivores, carnivores and omnivores
