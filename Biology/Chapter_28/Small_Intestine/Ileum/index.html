Ileum

Small Intestine / Ileum

The end products of Digestion


Food --> Digested To

Carbohydrats --> Monosaccharides (glucose)

Proteins --> Amino Acids

Lipids --> Fatty Acids and Glycerol

  • Walls made of villi and microvilli
  • The villi increase the surface area for absorption
  • Food is absorbed by diffusion

    • Adaptations made by the villi for absorption


  • Large numbers (increase surface area)
  • Large number of microvilli (increase surface area)
  • Their walls are only one cell thick
  • They have a rich blood supply
  • Capillaries in the villus absorb water and soluble nutrients [glucose, amino acids, vitamins, minerals
  • Capillaries carry nutrients to the hepatic portal vein which takes them to the liver
  • The liver acts as a warehouse storing and releasing nutrients
  • Amino acids cant be stored
  • Not used amino acids are broken down in the liver and form urea [deamination]
