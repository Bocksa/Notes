Small Intestine

Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Small Intestine

  • Has 2 main parts: the duodenum and the ileum
  • Duodenum carries out digestion, the ileum is the site of absorption

    • Adaptations of the small intestine for absorption

  • It is very long
  • It has numerous villi and microvilli
  • The walls of the villi are one cell thick
  • There is a rich blood supply to carry away water-soluble products
  • Each villus has a lymph supply to carry away the fats
