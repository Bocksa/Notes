Duodenum

Small Intestine / Duodenum

  • Carries out digestion [Primary Function]
  • Produces a range of digestive enzymes, produced by the cells lining it
  • Inner lining contains many infoldings called villi (has a velvety texture)
  • Each villus has many microvilli
  • The numerous infoldings increase the surface area for digestion
  • Intestinal glands between the villi produce a range of enzymes called intestinal juices
