Bile

Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Bile

  • Yellow-Green viscous liquid
  • Made of water, bile salts and bile pigments
  • Made in the liver and stored in the gall bladder
  • Enters duodenum via bile duct

    • Functions


  • Emulsified lipids
  • Neutralise chyme from the stomach
  • Excrete pigments made from damaged and red blood cells
