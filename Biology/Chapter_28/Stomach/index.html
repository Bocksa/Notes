Stomach

Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Stomach

  • J-Shaped muscular bag
  • Lining heavily folded producing millions of gastric glands
  • Gastric glands release gastric juice which contains mucus, pepsinogen and HCL
  • Mucus coats the stomach and prevents self digestion
  • Pepsiongen is an inactive enzyme and doesnt digest the cells in the stomach lining that produces it
  • Pepsin converts proteins to smaller peptides
  • Pepsin is a protease

Hydrochloric Acid (HCL) Functions

  1. Kills bacteria
  2. Activates pepsinogen
  3. Denatures amylase
  4. Loosens fibrous and cellular foods
  • Stomach contraction produces chyme (a thick, soupy mixture)
