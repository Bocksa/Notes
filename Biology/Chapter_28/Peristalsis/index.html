Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Peristalsis

  • Peristalsis is a wave of muscular action in the walls of the alimentary canal that moves the contents along

Role of Fibre

  • Prevents constipation by stimulating peristalsis
  • Pietry fibre (roughage) is made of cellulose
  • Humans cant digest cellulose
  • Fibre absorbs and stores water
  • Unabsorbed waste expands and stimulates peristalsis
