Digestion in the Mouth

Chapter 28 - Human Nutrition / Digestion in the Mouth

  • Mechanical digestion takes place in the mouth by chewing.
  • Chemical digestion occurs in the mouth by the actions of the enzyme amylase
  • Saliva contains water, salts, mucus, lycozone and amylase
  • Amylase works best at Ph 7-9
  • The Ph of the mouth is 7-9
