Vitro

Tissue Culture / Nervous Tissue

Consists of nerve cells called neurons.

Vitro Cells are grown outside the organism in an artificial
environment.

Vitro Cells are Grown in:

  • Glassware,
  • Bioreactor,
  • Bath

Necessary Conditions:

  • Growth Hormones,
  • Correct Temperature and Ph,
  • Sterile Environment,
  • Oxygen usually required
