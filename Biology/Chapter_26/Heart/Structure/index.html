The Heart / Structure of the Heart

  • Divided into 2 sides by a wall called the septum
  • The heart has 4 chambers

    • Atria

  • The 2 upper chambers are the atria (singular : atrium)
  • Pumps blood to ventricles
  • Has thin walls (pumps short distance)

    • Ventricles

  • The ventricles pump blood out of the heart
  • Right Ventricle pumps to the lungs
  • Left Ventricle pumps to the head and body
  • The left ventricle wall is thicker and stronger as blood travels further
